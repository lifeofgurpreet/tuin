# SEATING ZONE - GENERATION PROMPT

## Task

Generate a design visualization for the seating area of this garden.

## Reference Images (IN ORDER)

1. **Annotated space photos** - This is the ACTUAL garden. Your design MUST fit this space.
2. **Inspiration images** - Style references for seating furniture and layout.
3. **Layout drawing** (if provided) - Top-down view showing seating placement.

## Design Requirements

- Seating must fit within the actual garden space
- Position near shade structure for comfort
- Clear line of sight to play area (for supervising children)
- Natural materials preferred (wood, stone, rattan)

## Elements to Include

- Dining table with chairs (4-6 seats)
- Lounging area (optional)
- Side tables
- Ambient lighting (string lights, lanterns)

## DO NOT

- Block pathways or access points
- Place seating where it obstructs garden flow
- Use materials inconsistent with the garden style
